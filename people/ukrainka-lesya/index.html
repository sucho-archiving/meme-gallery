Ukrainka (Lesya)

Lesya Ukrainka was the penname of Larysa Kosach, a prominent Ukrainian author of the late 19th and early 20th centuries (link to her Wikipedia page below). She is an important figure in Ukrainian cultural mythology, and memes about Ukrainka will often invoke her alongside Taras Shevchenko, another key figure in Ukrainian cultural history.
