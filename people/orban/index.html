Orban

Viktor Orban has been the prime minister of Hungary since 2010 and has overseen a sharp turn to political and social conservatism in the country during his tenure (link to his Wikipedia page below). Among European leaders and heads of state of European Union countries, Orban has been among the most equivocal about Russia’s full-scale invasion of Ukraine. Memes about Orban will often imply Orban’s subservience to Russian President Vladimir Putin.
