Saldo (Vladimir)

Vladimir Saldo is a Ukrainian and Russian politician who was the mayor of Kherson from 2002 to 2012 and has been the installed governor of the Russian-occupied Kherson Oblast since October 2022 (link to his Wikipedia page below). Memes about Saldo tend to invoke a meeting of the governors of Russian-occupied territories in Ukraine with Russian President Vladimir Putin. Memes also comment on the fact that in November 2023 Saldo was sentenced in Ukraine to 15 years in prison (in absentia).
