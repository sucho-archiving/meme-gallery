Marin (Sanna)

Sanna Marin was the prime minister of Finland from 2019 to 2023 (link to her Wikipedia page below). During her tenure as prime minister, Marin oversaw the beginning of Finland’s joining of NATO, a move that angered Russia, as NATO would significantly expand its land borders with the Russian Federation. Memes about Marin will often point to the similarities between Finland’s desire to join NATO and Ukraine’s desire.
