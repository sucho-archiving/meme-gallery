Duda (Andrzej)

Andrzej Duda has been the president of Poland since 2015 (link to his Wikipedia page below). Since Russia’s full-scale invasion of Ukraine, Poland has emerged as, perhaps, Ukraine’s strongest supporter. Memes will often portray Duda alongside Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelensky, highlighting their close bond and Poland’s material and political support for Ukraine.
