Girkin (Strelkov)

Igor Girkin (also known by the alias Igor Stelkov) is a Russian veteran who played a role in Russia's seizure of Crimea and its war in the Donbas (link to his Wikipedia page below). He has called for a full mobilization in Russia in order to achieve victory in the war. Memes involving him often show him without his signature mustache, which he reportedly shaved in order to avoid recognition on the frontlines. He is often shown with a feminized appearance or in embarrassing situations. He is also often presented alongside other supporters of Russian President Vladimir Putin.
