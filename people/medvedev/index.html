Medvedev

Dmitrii Medvedev was the president of Russia from 2008 to 2012, the prime minister of Russian from 2012 to 2020, and the deputy chairman of the Security Council of Russia since 2020 (link to his Wikipedia page below). He has been a staunch supporter of Russian President Vladimir Putin and his full-scale war against Ukraine, expressing his support in frequent and incoherent posts in his Telegram channel. Memes about Medvedev will often present him as inadvertently humorous in his incoherence. Many memes present him along with other members of Putin’s cohort of advisers, oftentimes implying that all of those figures will meet a similar fate of prison or death. Memes will also often reference rumors about Medvedev’s drunkenness, stemming from claims that Medvedev was visibly intoxicated at the 2009 G8 summit.
