Macron

Emmanuel Macron has been the president of France since 2017, having won reelection in April 2022 (link to his Wikipedia page below). Memes about Macron will often point to his less than full-hearted support of Ukraine and its cause. He has made several controversial statements about Ukraine and Russia, such as saying that Russia should not be humiliated. Macron is also known for his frequent phone calls with foreign leaders, and his physically affectionate style with Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelensky. Macron is often presented alongside German Chancellor Olaf Scholz, who has been instrumental in withholding weapons from Ukraine.
