Chmut (Taras)

Taras Chmut is a Ukrainian veteran and a co-founder of the Ukraine Military Center who heads the foundation “Come Back Alive” (link to Wikipedia page on the foundation below). In his role as head of this foundation, Chmut makes regular calls for fundraising, and memes about him will often invoke this to humorous effect.
