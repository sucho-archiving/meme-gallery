Patron (dog)

Patron is a bomb detection dog and the mascot of the State Emergency Service of Ukraine (link to Wikipedia page below). Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelensky awarded Patron the Order of Courage for his work in detecting bombs left behind by Russian invaders.
