Nebenzya (Vasily)

Vasily Nebenzya is a Russian diplomat and the Russian ambassador to the United Nations (link to his Wikipedia page below). During the early days of Russia’s full-scale invasion of Ukraine, there were many tense and closely watched moments in the UN, and many criticized Nebenzya’s presence in the organization. Memes about Nebenzya will often invoke him in the UN and imply that he does not belong there, as the representative of a warring nation.
