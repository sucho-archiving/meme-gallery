Screaming Prigozhin

In the months leading to his mutiny in June 2023, Yevgeny Prigozhin recorded multiple videos in which he aggressively demanded ammunition to be sent to his Wagner private military group by Russia’s military command (usually the Russian Defense Minister, Sergei Shoigu, and the chief of the Russian Armed Forces, Valery Gerasimov). Screenshots of his screaming face and outstretched arm became a template for many memes. These memes usually contain text that begins with a question word “Where…?” (as in “Where is the ammunition?”)
