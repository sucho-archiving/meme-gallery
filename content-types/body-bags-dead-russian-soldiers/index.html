Body bags (dead Russian soldiers)

Memes involving body bags containing Russian soldiers are used to reflect the futility and failure of Russia's full-scale invasion of Ukraine. These images will sometimes show traditional body bags, sometimes simple trash bags, which connote that the amount of losses is so great that there are not enough body bags to accommodate the number. Memes including body bags will sometimes insert them into images associated with Russia, such as famous paintings or sites in Moscow's Red Square.
