Lukashenko (general)

Memes about Belarusian President Alexander Lukashenko are often mocking or demeaning in nature, invoking imagery of clowns or women's clothing frequently. Many memes show Lukashenko in relation to Russian President Vladimir Putin, whom he has supported throughout Russia's full-scale invasion of Ukraine. Lukashenko acted as a negotiator between Wagner leader Yevgeny Prigozhin and Russian President Vladimir Putin during the former's short-lived rebellion. After this incident, some memes began to appear about Lukashenko's role and his status a Putin's lackey. Memes will also often reference rumors about Lukashenko's health.
