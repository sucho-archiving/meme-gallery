Desintegration / disappearance of Russia in the future

Memes of this type often show maps in which the modern Russian Federation does not exist. In some, the territory is divided among different countries. In others, the territory is broken down into a large number of different republics. In some, the area simply does not exist. Memes of this type often invoke the division of Germany in the aftermath of World War II, placing Russia in the place of Nazi Germany.
