End of the Putin regime

Memes about the end of the reign of Russian President Vladimir Putin tend to paint one of two pictures about how this will come about: death or imprisonment. In memes connected to Putin's death, he is often shown alongside other bygone Russian or Soviet leaders. Gravestones are a common motif in these memes. In memes about his imprisonment, Putin is often shown in hiding or fleeing in the face of Ukraine's victory against Russia.
