Foreign fighters in Ukraine

Memes about foreign fighters in Ukraine often refer or allude to the Ukraine Foreign Legion (also known as the International Legion of Territorial Defence of Ukraine), which is made up of volunteers from abroad. Memes about this military branch often invoke children's storybook characters as volunteers in the legion.
