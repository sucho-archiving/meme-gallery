Zelensky visits USA (December 2022)

In December 2022, Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelensky undertook a surprise visit to the USA, his first trip outside of Ukraine since the start of Russia's full-scale invasion of the country. As CNN reports (linked below), this visit displayed the close ties between Zelensky and U.S. President Joe Biden. During this visit, Zelensky also addressed the U.S. Congress. Memes about this trip often show Zelensky with Biden or with then-Speaker of the House Nancy Pelosi.
