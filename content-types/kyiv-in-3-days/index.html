"Kyiv in 3 days"

In early February 2022, before the start of Russia’s full-scale invasion of Ukraine, Belarusian President Alexander Lukashenko predicted that Russian troops would be able to capture Kyiv in 3–4 days (link below). This, of course, did not come to pass, and memes invoking this propaganda tend to highlight the absurdity of the prediction and Russian propaganda in general.
