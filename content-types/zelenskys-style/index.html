Zelensky's style

Since the start of Russia's full-scale invasion of Ukraine, Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelensky has adopted an understated, casual style of dress. As The New York Times writes (linked below), his olive-green t-shirt has become something of a uniform for the wartime leader. Memes about Zelensky's style tend to highlight his humble clothing, occasionally in contrast to the overly expensive garments of Russian President Vladimir Putin.
