Vobla the dried fish - not a war ("voina"!)

A girl in Russia was acquitted of writing a pacifist slogan “Net v***e” ("No to war") after she was able to prove that the obscured word stood for “vobla” (i. e. vobla, the dried caspian roach fish) instead of "voina" ("war") (link to article below). Multiple memes feature the fish where the word “war” normally would be, for example, the cover of War and Peace (now Vobla and Peace).
