Z and/or V

'Z' or 'V' symbols have been associated with Russia in its war against Ukraine (link to explainer below). These letters have been seen on various military vehicles and equipment on the Russian side. Many memes display these letters as clear demarcations of the Russian military. Some memes will superimpose one or both of these letters on undesirable images, such as those of homelessness or failing infrastructure in Russia. At other times, the appendage of one or both of these symbols to various figures, such as Elon Musk, will serve as a form of accusation of support for Russia.
