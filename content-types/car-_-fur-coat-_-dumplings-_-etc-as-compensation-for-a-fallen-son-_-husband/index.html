Car / fur coat / dumplings / etc. as compensation for a fallen son / husband

The mothers and wives of dead Russian soldiers have been given such gifts as cars (first link below) and fur coats (second link below) as compensation for their dead family members. Memes invoking this trade imply that Russia places a very low value on human life, allowing for a substitution for fur coats or old cars. Some memes, furthermore, imply that some women in Russia encourage their husbands or sons to go to war in Ukraine so that they will receive these items.
