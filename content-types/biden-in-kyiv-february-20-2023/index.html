Biden in Kyiv (February 20 2023)

On February 20, 2023, American President Joe Biden made a surprise visit to Kyiv (link to news article below). Since the beginning of Russia's full-scale invasion of Ukraine, Biden has been an adamant supporter of Ukraine's cause. Memes about Biden's visit to Kyiv often display him in various locales around the city, such as pizza parlors or hat shops, implying that he is right at home in Kyiv. Some memes show Russian President Vladimir Putin viewing Biden's visit with disdain.
