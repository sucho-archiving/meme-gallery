Ukraine (general)

Memes concerning Ukraine generally praise the heroism and success of Ukraine in the face of Russia's full-scale invasion of the country. Many memes place Ukraine in contrast to Russia, with the former enjoying success and being motivated by loftier goals, while the latter is shown to be incompetent, corrupt, and without morality.
