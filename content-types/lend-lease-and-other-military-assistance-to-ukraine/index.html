Lend-Lease and other military assistance to Ukraine

Lend-Lease is a U.S. program that supplies weapons, food, oil, and other necessities to allies. The U.S. Congress passed this program in May 2022. Memes about this program and other military assistance to Ukraine tend either to express gratitude at that which has been received or to call for greater aid in the face of Russian aggression. Memes of this type sometimes criticize what is perceived as insufficient support for Ukraine, and Germany is a common target of such memes.
