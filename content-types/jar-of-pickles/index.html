Jar of pickles

Memes about a jar of pickles relate the story of a woman in Kyiv who claims she took down a drone by throwing a jar of pickles at it from her balcony. The woman later clarified that it was a jar of pickled tomatoes, not cucumbers, but memes about this narrative sometimes feature the original assumption of pickled cucumbers.
